The question is not "which model is best." It's "which model is best for this use case, with this data and this budget."

## When open source wins

When data cannot leave your infrastructure, when volume is massive, when you need to fine-tune with your domain.

## When it doesn't

When you need the immediate frontier, when you have no MLOps team, when the case is low-volume.
