# Quickstart: Dark Mode Contrast

## Purpose

Verify that the Ask section is readable and visually clear in dark mode.

## Manual Verification Steps

1. Open the landing page.
2. Switch to dark mode.
3. Inspect the Ask section badges.
4. Confirm badge labels are readable against the background.
5. Inspect the Ask search field.
6. Confirm the border is easy to notice and the text is readable.
7. Repeat the same check in light mode to confirm the layout remains consistent.

## Expected Result

- Badges are readable in dark mode.
- The search field border is visible in dark mode.
- Text inside the search field is readable in dark mode.
- Light mode remains usable and unchanged in layout.
