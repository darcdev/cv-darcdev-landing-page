# Quickstart: Static Nav Menu

## Verify the Change

1. Open the landing page in a browser.
2. Scroll down through the full page.
3. Confirm the navigation remains visible the whole time.
4. Check a narrow viewport and repeat the scroll test.
5. Open the mobile menu and confirm it still works while the nav stays visible.

## Expected Result

- The nav stays in view during scroll.
- The menu remains usable on mobile.
- The page design and spacing remain visually consistent.
